• Slice 3 or more large avocados and place in a large mixing bowl
  • Mince half of a red onion and add it to the large mixing bowl with the avocados
  • Mince or crush 3 cloves of garlic and add it to the mixing bowl
  • Seed and vein 1 jalapeno before mincing it and adding it to the mixing bowl
  • Add 2 tablespoons of Cumin, and 1 tablespoon of salt
  • Slice a lime in half, juice and add to the mixing bowl
  • Mix the bowl until it is mixed into a nice creamy guacamole for dipping or adding to tacos
