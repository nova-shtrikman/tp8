• Begin by taking your small mixing glass and putting 2 tablespoons of matcha powder into it.
  • Next, add a quarter-cup of water into the cup.
  • User your frother to froth the matcha powder together with the water.
  • Once the matcha is fully dissolved into the water, fill your pint-sized glass with ice.
  • Pour the matcha mixture over the ice.
  • Add a half-cup of vanilla almond milk into the cup.
  • Add a table spoon of vanilla sweet cream sweetener into the cup.
  • Use the straw to stir the mixture, until the whole drink is a light green colour.
  • Take your first sip and enjoy
